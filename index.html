What is Odo?

A simple app to log your drives and expenses. Enter odometer readings, track costs, export when needed.

  • Log trips with odometer readings
  • Optional GPS location snapshots
  • Track gas, tolls, parking, maintenance
  • Attach receipt photos
  • Manage multiple vehicles
  • Export to CSV or PDF
