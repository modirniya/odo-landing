Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

Summary

Odo is designed with privacy in mind. All your data stays on your device. We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your personal information.

Data Storage

All data you enter in Odo (trips, expenses, vehicles, receipts, and location snapshots) is stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your phone unless you explicitly export it.

Location Data

Odo can optionally capture a single GPS location snapshot when you log a trip or expense. This location is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to any server. You can use Odo without granting location permissions.

No Account Required

Odo does not require you to create an account. There is no sign-up, no login, and no personal information collected.

No Cloud Sync

Odo does not sync your data to any cloud service. Your data exists only on your device.

Data Export

You can export your data to CSV or PDF files at any time. These files are created locally and shared using your device's standard share functionality. We have no access to these exports.

Analytics

During the beta testing phase, we may collect anonymous crash reports and basic usage analytics through Google Play's built-in tools to help improve the app. This data does not include any of your trip, expense, or location information.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at contact@odometer.pro.
